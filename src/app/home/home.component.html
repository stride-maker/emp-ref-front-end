What is a Employee Referral ?

An employee recommendation program is coordinated and used by employers to ask current workers to recommend applicants for open positions in a formal program. Employee referral, unlike sourcing, is an internal process used to locate and recruit the best talent from the current networks of employees.


Ease of Employee Referral

Seeking talent was described as one of the main hiring obstacles, employee references are seen as one of the key outlets for finding high-quality career applicants by an overwhelming majority of modern recruiters. Optimizing the hiring techniques has become one of the top priorities for many businesses that want to employ and recruit more productively in today's hiring environment.
