Who is a Job Seeker?



Jobseeker refers to a person who is actively pursuing an opportunity/job for employment and has submitted an application to the employer. Typically, any job seeker applies with many online recruitment tools to boost career development prospects.


How Footsteps helps him?

Footsteps is a special portal targeting the referrer and the seeker. A partnership which holds the company's HR at a distance. We segregate and classify as per experience, location, skill sets all the referred employment jobs. Sets of techniques that allow a career seeker to find an effective job as per his preference in qualifications.























What is an interesting work for a jobseeker?

For the jobseeker, we describe interesting work as one that satisfies his skill set, and both employee and employer are benefited. While it is feasible, what is fascinating and important work for one person can be something different from the next individual with the same skill set. We describe the jobseeker as the person who wants to move jobs. They are searching for them. They may be fresher, working or unemployed, or they may simply like to change their work positions or places.

Footsteps is determined to support them in discovering the right strategies for their own career quest in their fascinating and important jobs.
