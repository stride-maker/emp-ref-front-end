Li'l bit about us..


Footsteps is an organized career site that offers a simplified forum for job seekers to uncover the secret positions alluded in their target organizations by company employees.
We act as an intermediate stage strives for excellence and keep sharing referred job opportunities on this platform and we do not exchange the referrer information to applicable applicants which restricts them from bugging referrer now and then. We enable Jobseeker to browse and apply jobs posted by employees of their dream company and track the application status.


How it works?

As per the job description, the referrer may post a referral filling out the necessary information required by the employer. Jobseeker will apply to these positions directly and monitor the progress of the request. In order to receive their employee referral incentive, the referrer can pick the candidate according to the company's qualification criteria and tap them on behalf of their current employer.
